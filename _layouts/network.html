The network below shows the full picture of all the nationalist parties and fascist movements in Europe and how they are connected. It contains 247 entities or nodes and 2813 connections or links. There are 70 nationalist parties with 153 links between them and 177 fascist movements with 2329 links between those. There are 27 parties linking to 48 fascist movements through 150 links. There are 88 movements linking to 24 parties through 181 links.

Use the dropdowns to select what meaning the colors and the size or the nodes in the network have. The colors can be used to show differentiate between countries, nationalist parties and fascist movements or algorithmically found communities of entities (more on that below). Use the size to get a feel for the importance of the nodes in the network. Select in degree to see how many nodes link to that party or out degree to show how many other nodes are linked to from node. Betweennes Centrality is a measure of how important a node is within a network in terms of information flow (more about that below). Hover over the nodes to get more detais on the node.

Side note: there are 41 different countries and 20 colors so one color represents multiple countries.
